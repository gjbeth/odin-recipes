Grilled Cheese

Description

This classic dish is quick, simple, and pairs perfectly with a warm bowl of tomato soup.

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Sliced cheese (chef's choice)
  • Salted butter

Recipe

  1. Remove two slices of bread from the loaf and smear butter on one side of each slice
  2. Place two to four slices of cheese on the un-buttered sides of the bread and join them together
  3. Place sandwich, butter side up, on a hot skillet
  4. Heat in skillet until both sides of the bread are golden brown and the cheese runs down the crust
  5. Enjoy!
