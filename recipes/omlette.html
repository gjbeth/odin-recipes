Omlette

Description

All you need is eggs, salt, pepper, and some heat! One of the easiest to make, but hardest to master, dishes around.

Ingredients

  • Three eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Unsalted butter

Recipe

  1. Smear butter onto skillet base
  2. Crack eggs into a skillet
  3. Generously sprinkle salt and pepper in the eggs and mix thoroughly with a kitchen utencil
  4. Heat on medium until the egg base is firm
  5. Gently roll the eggs and flip over
  6. Turn heat to low until the omlette is cooked through and is completely firm
  7. Enjoy!
